Personal Website

Welcome to my personal website! I originally became exposed to developing websites from my yearbook class. I used Wix to make my very first website. I loved creating that     website but wanted more control over the design. I had no coding experience so I went to codecademy to learn. Once I learnt the basics, I started coding my own website. As I
    created, I used Google searches to help me with specific details I wanted to add to my site. Showcased on my website are the about me section, my portfolio section which includes
    my photography, art, and other projects and the services section which includes my tutoring and photography services.

    Languages used for my website include HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
    I used Adobe Illustrator Draw to make my illustrations on my portfolio page and Adobe Lightroom to edit my pictures.

    I hope you enjoy browsing through my website and would love to hear from you!!
